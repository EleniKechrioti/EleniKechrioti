I am a Computer Science graduate and a passionate enthusiast in cybersecurity and digital forensics. I enjoy digging into how systems break, learning how to protect them, and exploring the fascinating world of tracing digital evidence.

* **Currently:** Pursuing my Master's (MSc) in Information Systems Security & Development at [AUEB](https://mscis.cs.aueb.gr/en/normal/home).
* **Background:** BSc in Computer Science at AUEB.
* **Academic Work:** Completed my thesis on evaluating **Blockchain's role in enhancing Capability-Based Access Control** within Industrial IoT (IIoT) environments.
* **Cybersecurity Enthusiast:** Active in CTFs and platforms like **TryHackMe** & **CryptoHack**. Deeply interested in Applied Cryptography, Penetration Testing, and SIEM concepts.
* **Digital Forensics:** Gained hands-on academic experience in memory and disk analysis, tracing artifacts, and exploring tools like Autopsy and Volatility through practical assignments.
* **Currently researching/learning:** Exploring in-memory malware analysis and volatile memory forensics to detect and trace advanced threats.
